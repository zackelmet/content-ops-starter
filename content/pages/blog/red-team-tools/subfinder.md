# Subfinder

**Category:** redteam-tools  
**URL:** [https://github.com/projectdiscovery/subfinder](https://github.com/projectdiscovery/subfinder)  

Discover the power of Subfinder, your go-to tool for passive subdomain discovery. In an ever-evolving digital landscape, knowing your organization's vulnerabilities is crucial. Subfinder helps you uncover hidden subdomains that could be potential entry points for cyber threats. By leveraging passive data sources, it enables you to gather intelligence without triggering alerts. Perfect for security professionals and researchers, Subfinder streamlines your reconnaissance efforts, making it easier to assess your attack surface. Stay one step ahead of cyber adversaries and fortify your defenses with Subfinder. Your organization's security starts with visibility.
