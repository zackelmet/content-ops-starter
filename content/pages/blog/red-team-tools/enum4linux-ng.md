# Enum4linux-ng

**URL:** [https://github.com/cddmp/enum4linux-ng](https://github.com/cddmp/enum4linux-ng)  

Unlock the potential of your network security with Enum4linux-ng, the advanced SMB enumeration tool designed for comprehensive information gathering. This powerful tool enables security professionals to extract critical data from Windows systems, providing insights into users, shares, and more. By leveraging its capabilities, you can identify vulnerabilities before they become exploitable threats.

Enum4linux-ng is essential for penetration testers and system administrators alike, streamlining the process of assessing network security. With its user-friendly interface and robust functionality, you can enhance your security posture and ensure your systems remain resilient against potential attacks. Stay one step ahead in the cybersecurity game with Enum4linux-ng as your go-to enumeration solution.
