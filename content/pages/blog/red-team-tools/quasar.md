# Quasar

**URL:** [https://github.com/quasar/Quasar](https://github.com/quasar/Quasar)  

Introducing Quasar, the lightweight Windows Command and Control tool designed for seamless integration with Linux infrastructure. This powerful solution enables users to manage and execute commands on Windows systems remotely, all while maintaining a minimal footprint. 

Quasar is perfect for security professionals looking to enhance their cybersecurity measures or for ethical hackers conducting penetration tests. Its versatility allows for easy deployment in various environments, ensuring that you have the controls you need at your fingertips. Elevate your security strategy with Quasar and ensure your systems remain protected against potential threats.
