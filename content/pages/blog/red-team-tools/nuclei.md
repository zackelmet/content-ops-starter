# Nuclei

**URL:** [https://github.com/projectdiscovery/nuclei](https://github.com/projectdiscovery/nuclei)  

Introducing Nuclei, the powerful vulnerability scanner that leverages templates to identify security weaknesses in your applications. Whether you are a developer or a security professional, Nuclei simplifies the process of scanning for vulnerabilities, allowing you to focus on what matters most—keeping your systems secure. 

With its customizable templates, Nuclei can adapt to your specific needs, making it an essential tool for proactive security assessments. Discover vulnerabilities before they become problems and ensure your applications are fortified against threats. Don’t leave your security to chance; harness the power of Nuclei and stay one step ahead in the cybersecurity game.
