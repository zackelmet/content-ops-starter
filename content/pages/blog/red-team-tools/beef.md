# BeEF

**URL:** [https://github.com/beefproject/beef](https://github.com/beefproject/beef)  

Discover BeEF, the powerful browser exploitation framework designed for penetration testing and security assessments. With its focus on cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks, BeEF allows security professionals to assess the vulnerabilities of web applications and enhance their defense strategies. 

Utilizing a command and control server, this tool enables users to hook browsers and execute various exploits, turning the browser into an attack vector. Whether you are a security researcher or an ethical hacker, BeEF helps you understand how attackers could manipulate web technologies, giving you the insights needed to strengthen your cybersecurity posture. Dive into the world of browser-based threats and arm yourself with the knowledge to protect your digital assets.
