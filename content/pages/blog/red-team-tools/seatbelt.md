# Seatbelt

**URL:** [https://github.com/GhostPack/Seatbelt](https://github.com/GhostPack/Seatbelt)  

Introducing Seatbelt, the ultimate tool for system enumeration post-compromise. This powerful solution allows cybersecurity professionals to gather critical information about system configurations and potential vulnerabilities after an incident has occurred. With Seatbelt, you can quickly assess the security posture of compromised systems and identify lingering threats that could jeopardize your network.

Utilizing a comprehensive set of checks, Seatbelt provides insights into user accounts, services, and security policies. This information is essential for understanding the extent of a breach and formulating an effective remediation strategy. Empower your incident response efforts with Seatbelt and stay one step ahead of cyber threats.
