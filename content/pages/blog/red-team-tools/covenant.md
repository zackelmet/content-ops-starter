# Covenant

**URL:** [https://github.com/cobbr/Covenant](https://github.com/cobbr/Covenant)  

Introducing Covenant, the powerful C# .NET-based command and control (C2) framework designed for penetration testers and security professionals. With its intuitive interface and robust capabilities, Covenant streamlines the process of managing compromised systems while giving users full control over their testing environment. 

Whether you are simulating advanced persistent threats or conducting red team exercises, Covenant provides the tools you need to execute your strategies effectively. Its modular architecture supports custom payloads and allows for seamless integration with other security tools, making it a versatile asset in your cybersecurity toolkit. Elevate your security assessments and stay ahead of potential threats with Covenant.
