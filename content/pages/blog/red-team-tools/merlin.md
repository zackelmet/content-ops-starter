# Merlin

**URL:** [https://github.com/Ne0nd0g/merlin](https://github.com/Ne0nd0g/merlin)  

Introducing Merlin, the cutting-edge cybersecurity tool designed for seamless command and control operations over HTTP/2. Built with Golang, Merlin provides a robust framework for managing and deploying remote agents with unparalleled efficiency. Its advanced protocol support ensures that communications remain stealthy and resilient against detection.

Ideal for penetration testers and red teamers, Merlin streamlines the process of executing tasks on compromised systems. With its user-friendly interface and powerful capabilities, you can focus on what matters most: securing your network and identifying vulnerabilities. Elevate your cybersecurity strategy with Merlin and stay one step ahead of potential threats.
