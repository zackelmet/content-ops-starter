# SharpHound

**URL:** [https://github.com/BloodHoundAD/SharpHound](https://github.com/BloodHoundAD/SharpHound)  

Introducing SharpHound, the powerful Active Directory data collection tool designed to enhance your cybersecurity strategy. With its ability to gather comprehensive data on user permissions and relationships within your network, SharpHound enables organizations to identify potential vulnerabilities and mitigate risks effectively. 

By visualizing the intricate connections in your Active Directory, SharpHound helps security teams understand attack paths and prioritize remediation efforts. It is an essential tool for proactive threat hunting and ensures that your network remains secure against evolving cyber threats. Elevate your security posture with SharpHound and stay one step ahead of potential attackers.
